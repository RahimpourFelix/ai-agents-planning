## Collection and storage of personal data, nature, and purpose of their use

a) When visiting the website

When you visit our website, we will collect information on our servers, including:

- IP address of the requesting computer
- date and time of access
- name and URL of the retrieved files
- referrer URL
- the used browser, if applicable, the operating system, access provider, and country of request

We process the data for these purposes:

- ensuring a smooth connection to our offer
- ensuring a comfortable use of our offer
- evaluating system security and reliability
- for further administrative uses.

The legal basis for data processing is Art. 6 para. 1 p. 1 lit. f GDPR.
Our legitimate interest follows from the data collection purposes listed above.
In no case do we use the collected data to conclude you.
In addition, we use cookies and analytics services when you visit our website.

b) When using our application

When you use our application, we will store additional information about you, including:

- Name
- E-mail address
- Address
- Payment information

In addition, we store personal information in our systems.

The storage is based on our legitimate interests, as well as the user’s protection against misuse and other unauthorized use. 
A transfer of these data to third parties does not take place, 
unless it is necessary for the prosecution of our claims or there is 
a legal obligation in accordance with the law. Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR. 

When an account is closed, we will remove all related data after thirty days.

The personal user data processed in the context of this online offer includes inventory data (e.g., names and addresses of customers), contract data (e.g., services used, names of clerks, payment information), usage data (e.g. websites visited on our online offer, interest in our products), and content data (e.g. entries in the contact form).

“Users” includes all categories of persons affected by data processing. This includes, for example, our business partners, customers, interested parties, and other visitors to our online offering.

## Cookies

We use cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored locally in the cache of your Internet browser.

Cookies enable the recognition of the Internet browser. This does not mean we can identify you.

The files help the browser navigate the online offer and use the full range of functions.

The data processed by cookies is necessary for the aforementioned purposes to protect our legitimate interests and those of third parties in accordance with 
Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR.

Most browsers accept cookies automatically. You can configure your browser
so that no cookies are stored on your computer. However, deactivating 
the use of cookies may prevent the use of our feature offer.
